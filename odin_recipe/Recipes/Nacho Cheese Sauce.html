Nacho Cheese Sauce

Description

This nacho cheese sauce recipe makes a simple sauce that's delicious spread over tortilla chips. Add some sliced jalapeños to spice things up a bit.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 7 slices processed American cheese
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour until a paste forms. Pour in milk and stir until mixture thickens.
  2. Add cheese and salt; cook and stir until cheese has melted, about 15 minutes.
